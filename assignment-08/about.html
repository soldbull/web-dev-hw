Reason for Choosing this Instructable


Why did I choose this project and what does it mean for me?

I chose this project because I value my heritage and value my tribal identity. I am Salish and Crow and in these ways, long hair is cherished. It has meanings in different aspects of our lives. Both males and females are encouraged to grow their hair long, as a sign of their tribal heritage and cultural values. I feel that reminding my children and the people in our environment that we are unique. In this way, long hair is acceptable and a way of life.

Where can you learn more?

It just so happens that this empowerment has been a mega-trend throughout Indian Country. You only need to search boys with braids to find more information about the pride that Indigenous people have in promoting their cultural heritage and their values in showing children to grow their hair long.
