Troll the World with Positivity

By, James L. Walpole

When everyone expects you to be negative, be positive.
When everyone expects you to stop, speed up.
When everyone expects your bitter defeat, be festive.
When everyone is sitting it out, dance like a fool.
When everyone is terrified, be courageous.
When everyone expects destruction, start hoping.
When everyone is turning inward, be kind.

How to Think Through Losing a Lost Shoe

By, Ann Beattie

Were you swimming, and is another shoe also lost?
Do you have a dog?
Do you ever secretly gnaw on the sole of your shoe? If so, might you have eaten the entire shoe?
Might it have disappeared down the same drain from which the bit of dental floss protruded?
Are you not aware that drains vary in size?
If, as John Lennon suggested, you can imagine there being no heaven, can you imagine that you never had the shoe?
